London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.


London has a diverse range of cultures and has more than 300 languages being spoken in Great London. There are many places in which people can go and have a wonderful time. People can go to the world-famous British Museum. Also, people can see the London tower which is very interesting.
