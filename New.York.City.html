New York City

New York City is a city in the state of New York. It is the most populous city in the U.S. It is also the center of the New York Metropolitan area, the premier gateway for legal immigration to the United States.


New York City is an important center for international diplomacy and has been described as the cultural and financial capital of the world. In 2013, it was hosted as the third most visited tourist attractions.
