Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a city in Southern California famed as the center of the nation's film and television industry. It is the second-largest city in the United States after New York City, the most populous city in the state of California, and the county seat of Los Angeles County.


Los Angeles is known for its mediterranean climate and ethnic diversity. The city's inhabitants are referred to as Angelenos.
